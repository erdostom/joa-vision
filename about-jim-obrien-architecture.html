About Jim O’Brien Architecture & Interiors

At Jim O’Brien Architecture & Interiors we work to improve our clients’ well-being through design excellence, sensitive client service, and a strong sense of local responsibility. Our projects encompass a diverse mix of commercial and residential developments. We serve our clients by following these simple principles: we truly listen, and we closely manage an interactive design and construction process.
